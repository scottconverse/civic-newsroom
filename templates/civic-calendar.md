## How to Find This Information

Most cities publish their meeting calendar at one of these locations:
- City Clerk's page
- "Government" or "Your Government" section of city website
- Agenda management portal (PrimeGov, Granicus, Municode, Legistar)
- "Boards & Commissions" page

If you can't find it online, call your City Clerk's office. Meeting schedules are public information.

---

## Aggregation Timing Recommendations

- **Best days to run the News Aggregator:** The day after council meetings, and 2-3 days before upcoming meetings (when packets are posted)
- **Best days to run the Black Desk:** Mondays (catch up on weekend community discourse) or Fridays (scan for items buried before the weekend)
- **Best time to check for new sources:** After major meetings when new organizations, consultants, or agencies are referenced in packets
